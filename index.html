About Bookri

Bookri is a revolutionary app designed to modernize the traditional Kuri system. With Bookri, users can create or join Kuri groups, manage contributions, and track progress seamlessly, all from the comfort of their devices.

Easy Group Creation

Create Kuri groups with ease and invite members to join instantly.

Transparent Tracking

Track contributions, draws, and progress with full transparency.

Secure Transactions

Ensure safe and secure financial management for all members.
